Full Content Automation Pipeline

From raw idea to live post on LinkedIn & Instagram — zero manual steps after approval.

💡
Step 1 — Idea Generation AI
A scheduled job (Inngest cron, daily 7am) scrapes trending CA/finance/AI topics from Reddit, LinkedIn, Twitter, and RBI/ICAI announcements. Claude ranks them by relevance + engagement potential and queues 7 ideas for the week automatically.

Step 2 — Multi-Format Draft Creation AI
Claude generates 3 formats simultaneously from one idea: (1) LinkedIn post — hook + insight + CTA, 1200 chars, your tone. (2) Instagram caption — punchy, emoji-rich, hashtags included. (3) WhatsApp community drop — conversational, no fluff, under 200 words. All three stored in your content DB.
Claude API System Prompt (your tone) Supabase content table
Draft + visual request sent to design engine

Step 3 — Visual / Carousel Generation AI
For Instagram: auto-generates a 5-slide carousel using a pre-built Canva template via Canva API (or html-to-image for custom branded cards). Key stats and points from the post become slide text. For LinkedIn: optional 1080×1080 branded image auto-generated. No Photoshop, no Canva manually.
Canva API html-to-image Cloudflare R2 (already set up)
Draft lands in your 2-minute approval queue

Step 4 — Quick Approval Dashboard 1 Touch
A simple internal web page (built into Planora or standalone) shows you the week's 7 drafted posts. You see the LinkedIn post, Instagram caption, and image side-by-side. Three buttons: Approve → Schedule, Edit, or Reject. Approving one post takes under 10 seconds. Do all 7 in one Sunday sitting (under 5 minutes total).

On approval, the post is automatically scheduled via API at the best posting times: LinkedIn (Tue–Thu, 8–9am or 6–7pm IST), Instagram (Mon/Wed/Fri, 7pm IST). No Buffer needed — direct API calls from your own system. You own the scheduling logic.

Step 6 — Auto-Post to LinkedIn LinkedIn API
LinkedIn Marketing API (UGC Posts endpoint) publishes the post with image directly to your profile or company page. Supports text posts, image posts, and article shares. Auth via OAuth 2.0 — one-time setup, runs forever. Posts as you, not a third-party tool watermark.

Meta Graph API (Instagram Business) publishes carousel or single image posts with caption + hashtags. Requires Instagram Business account linked to a Facebook Page — one-time setup. Supports feed posts and carousels. Reels need a video file (can auto-generate screen-recording style with ffmpeg).
Meta Graph API Instagram Business account Facebook Page (required) Long-lived access token

48 hours after posting, a cron job pulls engagement data (likes, comments, shares, reach) from both APIs. Data stored in Supabase. Claude analyzes which post formats, topics, and hooks performed best — and uses that to score next week's idea queue higher. The system gets smarter every week.

YouTube — Full Analysis

Scored on success probability, ROI, and time-to-returns. Honest numbers, no hype.

7.5
/10
Overall Score
Strong long-term play. Wrong time to start it.
YouTube is the highest-trust, highest-compounding content channel available. A 2-year-old video can still bring in leads every day. But it takes 8–12 months of consistent output before it meaningfully contributes to revenue — and it's the most time-intensive channel. The smart move: automate Shorts from Day 1 using your existing content pipeline, and start long-form at Month 3 when Planora is more hands-off and you have workshop recordings to repurpose.
✅ Why It Works for You
  • CA + AI on YouTube is wide open in India — almost no direct competition
  • Sachit on camera = instant credibility. CAs trust other CAs, not tech bros
  • Workshop recordings = free long-form content. You're already making it.
  • YouTube SEO: "GST filing with AI", "CA automation" — high intent, low competition
  • Compounding: videos from Month 3 still bring leads at Month 24
  • Shorts can be 100% auto-generated from your LinkedIn pipeline (n8n)
  • Planora demo videos = product marketing + YouTube content simultaneously
⚠️ The Real Challenges
  • 8–12 months before YouTube meaningfully drives revenue. Long patience test.
  • Long-form editing: 3–4 hrs/video DIY. Must outsource to stay sane.
  • Algorithm requires consistency — missing 2 weeks resets momentum
  • Indian CA audience skews WhatsApp-first, YouTube second. Not their primary platform.
  • AdSense revenue will be low (Indian CPM ₹30–80). Not the monetization path.
  • Camera presence needs to be developed. First 20 videos will feel uncomfortable.
  • Thumbnail design matters massively. Needs a system or a designer.
💸 One-Time Setup Costs
  • Mic: Rode NT-USB Mini or Blue Yeti — ₹8,000–12,000
  • Lighting: Elgato Key Light or ring light — ₹5,000–8,000
  • Camera: Your phone on a ₹2,000 tripod works fine to start
  • Background: Clean wall or basic bookshelf. ₹0.
  • Editing software: DaVinci Resolve (free) or give to editor
  • Total one-time: ₹15,000–22,000. Low barrier.
⏱ Weekly Time Cost (Ongoing)
  • Shorts (auto): 0 hrs — n8n generates from LinkedIn posts
  • Long-form script: 45–60 mins (Claude drafts it, you edit)
  • Recording: 20–30 mins per video
  • Editing: 0 hrs — outsource to editor (₹2,000–3,000/video)
