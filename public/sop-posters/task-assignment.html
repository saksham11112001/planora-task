01

Create a Task

Go to Tasks (or open any Project) → click + New Task. Add a title, description, due date, and priority.

Assign to a Team Member

Pick an assignee from your team. Toggle Approval Required if you want to review the work before it's marked done.

Assignee Works on the Task

Rahul sees the task in My Tasks. He can add comments, attach files, and update status as he progresses.

04

Manager Gets Notified Instantly

You receive an email with one-click Approve / Reject buttons — no need to log in to upFloat.

Approve or Reject in One Click

Click Approve in the email — the task is marked done automatically. Click Reject — it goes back to Rahul with a notification to fix and resubmit.
