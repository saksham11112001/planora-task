03

Understand the Role Hierarchy

upFloat has 5 roles. Pick the right one for each team member to control what they can see and do.

RoleWhat they can do
OwnerFull access. Billing, settings, delete org.
AdminAll features. Cannot change billing.
ManagerCreate + assign tasks. Approve work.
MemberWork on assigned tasks. View clients.
ViewerRead-only. Cannot create or edit anything.
04

Assign Tasks to Team Members

Once the team is set up, assign tasks directly to individuals. Each person has a personalised My Tasks view showing only their work.

Customise Permissions Per Role

In Settings → Permissions, fine-tune exactly what each role can do — e.g. allow Members to assign tasks, restrict Managers from deleting clients.
